# Product Spine (v0)

## Who this is for
Individuals who feel overwhelmed managing important parts of their life across disconnected tools.

## What core problem does it solve? 
People waste time and mental energy managing their life because information and actions are fragmented across tools and require too much manual effort.

## What must always remain true, no matter how fast we move?
- Capturing information must feel effortless
- The user should never feel forced into structure
- The system should adapt to the user, not the other way around
